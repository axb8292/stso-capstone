About me and special thanks!

About me

I am Abby Brooks, a Mechanical Engineer and six year aquarium enthusiast. From the time I was 7 years of age, my family has had aquariums in our home. I took a full nose dive into the hobby during high school with a personal guppy breeding project and a goldfish research project. Since that time I have not only exponentially increased my number of aquariums but also my knowledge. The majority of my free time consists of listening to hobby podcasts or browsing YouTube on caring for different home species. I currently have 13 aquariums and plan on increasing to 15 after moving. I currently run a fish breeding business and sell to local hobbyists as well as adopt unwanted betta fish. I house three betta fish, two varieties of bristlenose plecos, one variety of common pleco, a variety of fancy guppies, and two varieties of neocaridina shrimp.
